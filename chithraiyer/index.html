Chitra Iyer

Chitra Iyer, also known as Chitra Sivaraman, is a well-known Indian playback singer. She was born in Kerala and raised in Delhi, India, and began her singing career in the early 1990s. Chitra’s melodious voice and unique style quickly gained her recognition and popularity in the Indian music industry. Over the years, Chitra has lent her voice to numerous hit songs in several Indian languages, including Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, and Malayalam. Some of her most popular Tamil songs include “Ale Ale” from “Boys”, Athini Sithini from “Tenali”, “Nenthikittein” from “Star which are composed by A. R Rahman. And also famous Malayalam songs “Chundathu Chethippoo” from the movie “chronic bachelor”,”Ishtamalleda” from movie “Swapnakoodu”, “Mattupetti” from the movie “mayilattam”.
